The target audience for Beautiful Idaho Sky will be people and families (mainly from the 3 towns in Idaho) interested in current weather conditions, meteorology, photography, community stories, and in promoting unity in the community.

Personas

Jane Doe

  • Job title: Homemaker
  • Demographics: 48 years old, married, mother of four teenage children, one of which is engaged, has a bachelor of science degree and is currently working on a masters degree.
  • Environment: She uses her desktop computer intermittently throughout the day to check emails, find recipes, post on social media, and prepare lessons for her church calling. about 2 to 3 hours in all and uses her smart phone when she leaves the house. She is connected to the internet via wireless modem at home and LTE wireless mobile when out. Her kids own a tablet but she hardly ever uses it.
  • Goals and tasks: Devoted to caring for her family and home, spends time cooking cleaning, planning, and serving. She cares about planning meaningful family excursions and connecting with the community.

John Doemight

  • Job title: Accountant
  • Demographics: 24 years old, un-married, recent college graduate, salary is close to 35,000/year.
  • Environment: He has a school laptop but rarely uses it now that he has graduated. He mainly uses his smart phone or tablet to brows the internet.
  • Goals and tasks: He is eager to establish himself in his newly chosen career. He is actively saving up to buy a starter home and is currently dating a girl he met in his college class. He recently bought new lenses for his DSLR camera and enjoys photographing nature as a hobby. He also wants to plan exciting dates to take his girlfriend on, usually involving hiking, skiing or other outdoor activities.
