Site Purpose:

A platform for a tight knit community of weather enthusiasts in three towns in south-east Idaho who want to review current weather condition data, stay in-the-loop with local news, see current weather stories and pictures, gather at the special bi-annual events, and participate in the bi-annual photo competition. Our goal is to inspire future scientists and photographers, and help build a tight knit community.
