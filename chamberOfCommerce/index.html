The Mesa Chamber of Commerce

Who are we and what is our mission?

We are a local association of people devoted to the growth and prosperity of Mesa, AZ. We work together to build a strong economy, and community by strengthening and supporting small businesses, help grow our unique industries, improve education and transportation, and making Mesa, AZ the best place to work and live.

Why join our Chamber?

As a member you have the opportunity to voice your ideas, be involved in upcoming plans, and receive greater business exposure. You'll have a company vote in the public affairs committee and have access to the support of our growing network of chamber members. You'll receive special invitations to ribbon-cutting ceremonies honoring expansions and grand openings and attend the yearly gala. Joining the Mesa Chamber of Congress is the best way to invest in your business, your community and your future!
