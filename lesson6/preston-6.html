A Great Place to Take the Family Sledding

Preston Mountain is one of the best places to let the kids zoom down slick hills on sleds and inner tubes. It even has teeny moguls for tots and lots of room for building snow men. You can purchase plastic snow sleds and on weekends and major holidays there are concessions that sell snacks, drinks and hot chocolate. Parking is convenient within a short walking distance to play areas. If you like snowshoeing, strap on the gear and get going.
